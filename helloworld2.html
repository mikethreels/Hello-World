before we start building anything we first need to buy a piece of land
and offcourse get the right papers

what we need for our Foundation

  • Beton
  • Building blocks
  • Styrofoam
  • Excavator
  • Tar

What we need to do

  1. dig a hole as big as you need
  2. putting boards together where the Foundation will be
  3. pouring the Beton
  4. covering the foundation in tar
  5. building the walls of the foundation
  6. covering the inside of the walls witht tar
  7. insulating the outside of the walls with styrofoam
  8. covering the styrofoam with tar
